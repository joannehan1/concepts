내가 어렸을 때, 어느 날 밤에 자다 깼다.

One night, when I was younger, I suddenly woke up from my sleep.

 

서울 아파트에 내가 우리 할머니랑 자고있었다.

I was sharing a bed with my grandmother in her apartment in Seoul.

 

그런데, 갑자기, 침대에서 구르면서 떨어졌다.

But, suddenly, I rolled across the bed and fell off.

 
 
 
 

다행히 다치지 않았다.

Luckily, I wasn't hurt.

 

할머니 침대 옆에 바닥에 옷이 쌓여 있어서 그곳에 착륙했다.

There was a pile of clothes on the floor next to her bed, which I had landed on.

 

소리 때문에 할머니도 깼다. 처음엔 걱정을 했었는데, 내가 다치지 않아서 괜찮았다.

The noise woke up my grandmother – who was worried when she first saw me – but when she realized I was not hurt, she was relieved.

 

같이 새벽에 일어나서, 창문 밖의 교회 십자가를 세었다.

We both got up, and counted the church crosses outside the window until dawn.
